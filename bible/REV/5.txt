     Na mehunuu nwoma bi wɔ deɛ ɔte ahennwa no soɔ no nifa mu a wɔatwerɛ emu ne akyire, na wɔde nsɔano nson asosɔ ano.  Na mehunuu ɔbɔfoɔ hoɔdenfoɔ bi a ɔde nne kɛseɛ teaa mu sɛ: Hwan na ɔfata sɛ ɔbue nwoma no mu na ɔte ne nsɔano no?  Na obiara nni soro anaa asase so anaa asase ase a ɔbɛtumi abue nwoma no mu anaasɛ ɔbɛhwɛ mu.  Na mesuu pii, ɛfiri sɛ wɔnhunu obi a ɔfata sɛ ɔbɛbue nwoma no mu anaasɛ ɔbɛhwɛ mu.   Na mpanimfoɔ no mu baako ka kyerɛɛ me sɛ: Nsu, hwɛ, Yuda abusuakuo mu Gyata no, Dawid nhini no adi nkonim sɛ ɔbɛbue nwoma no mu na watete ne nsɔano nson no.      Na mehwɛe, na ahennwa no ne ateasefoɔ baanan no ntam ne mpanimfoɔ no ntam no, adwammaa bi gyina hɔ a ɔte sɛdeɛ wɔaku no. Ɔwɔ mmɛn nson ne ani nson a ɛyɛ Nyankopɔn ahonhom nson a wɔasoma wɔn kɔ asase nyinaa soɔ no.  Na ɔba bɛgyee nwoma no firii deɛ ɔte ahennwa no soɔ no nifa mu.      Na ɔgyee nwoma no no, ateasefoɔ baanan no ne mpanimfoɔ aduonu nan no hwehwee ase adwammaa no anim, wɔn nyinaa kurakura nsankuo ne sika mfamfa a ohwam a ɛyɛ ahotefoɔ mpaebɔ no ayɛ no mama.   Na wɔtoo dwom foforɔ sɛ: Wofata sɛ wogye nwoma no na wobue ne nsɔano no, ɛfiri sɛ wɔaku wo, na wode wo mogya atɔ yɛn ama Onyankopɔn, afiri mmusua ne ɔkasa ne nkurɔfoɔ ne aman nyinaa mu,      na woayɛ wɔn ahemman ne asɔfoɔ ama yɛn Nyankopɔn, na wɔbɛdi asase so ahene.      Na mehwɛe, na metee abɔfoɔ bebree a wɔatwa ahennwa no ne ateasefoɔ no ne mpanimfoɔ no ho kɔntɔnkrɔn nne, wɔn ano si mpempem ne mpem ahodoɔ mpem,   na wɔde nne kɛseɛ kaa sɛ: Adwammaa a wɔkumm no no fata sɛ ɔgye tumi, ne ahonya, ne nyansa, ne ahoɔden, ne anidie, ne animuonyam ne nhyira.   Na abɔdeɛ a ɛwɔ soro ne asase soɔ ne asase ase ne ɛpo so ne deɛ ɛwɔ mu nyinaa, metee sɛ wɔse: Nhyira ne anidie ne animuonyam ne tumi nka deɛ ɔte ahennwa no soɔ, ne adwammaa no daa daa!      Na ateasefoɔ baanan no kaa sɛ: Amen. Na mpanimfoɔ no hwehwee ase, na wɔkoto sɔree.
